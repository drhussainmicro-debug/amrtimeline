How to use this timeline
✎ Edit anythingClick Edit, then change the title, section names, activities, dates and % complete. The chart updates as you type.
⚑ Flag what's slippingClick the flag on any row to mark it late / needs attention. Anything past its end date and under 100% turns red automatically.
⚑ Focus the viewUse Late / flagged to hide everything except the items that need action.
💾 Keep your versionClick Download / save copy to save an HTML file you can re-open later or email to colleagues — no software needed, it runs in any browser.

Install on iPhone / iPad

  1. Tap the Share icon in Safari (the square with an up-arrow).
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add — the AMR Timeline icon appears on your home screen.
